Make agents emit an ISO-8601 UTC timestamp with a delta-since-previous-stamp at phase transitions and long-running operations (NOT every trivial line), so the operator can see elapsed duration between work outputs. TWO surfaces: (a) intercom-style progress broadcasts carry the stamp (agent-intercom Progress Protocol overlay), and (b) a general universal instruction rule so agents emit the stamp regardless of the intercom pack. Templates are the product: primary change is a new universal instruction template plus an agent-intercom Progress Protocol extension, with installed .github/instructions mirrors updated to match (autoharness dogfoods its harness).
