Add a new step to install-harness SKILL.md (after Step 1.3 and before Step 1.3b) for community template selection. The step should: (1) Read templates/community/registry.yaml from autoharness_home. (2) For each entry, evaluate applicable_profiles against the workspace profile's detected languages, frameworks, and stack packs. (3) Check prerequisite_packs — only propose templates whose prerequisites are satisfied by the selected capability_packs. (4) Produce a ranked list of applicable community templates. (5) Present the list to the operator for opt-in selection (community templates are never auto-installed). (6) Selected templates are copied to the target workspace following the same artifact-class placement rules as first-party templates. (7) Record selected community templates in the harness manifest under a new community_templates[] array. Also add a row to the install-layer table indicating that community templates are part of the 'overlays' layer.
