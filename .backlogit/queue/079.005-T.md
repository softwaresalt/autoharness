Add an autoharness-owned reporting surface that reads local telemetry sinks and produces deterministic time-series summaries for trends, regressions, cost/usage efficiency, offload, and gap detection.

<!-- BEGIN:acceptance-criteria -->
- Reports can summarize a selected date/range or task/session slice from local SQLite and/or JSONL data under `.autoharness/metrics/`.
- Trend output includes token consumption, token generation, context-area estimates, COGS, duration, routed-vs-raw usage, avoided-read evidence, and gap counts.
- Output distinguishes observed, estimated, derived, and unavailable measurements.
- The CLI/library degrades gracefully when telemetry is disabled, the DB is absent, or only partial v1.0 records exist.
<!-- END:acceptance-criteria -->

<!-- BEGIN:implementation-notes -->
Autoharness owns this local time-series reporting surface. Backlogit may link to reports later but should not become the metric store. Do not require an external service.
<!-- END:implementation-notes -->
